Assignment 5: Music with Markov Chains

A race between 10 cars. After the end of every race, the each individual in the population of cars is evaluated for its "fitness" (how far it traveled). A mating pool is chosen in a roulette wheel fashion, where a car's chance of being chosen is proportional to its fitness. The cars then "reproduce" by creating children with a mix of both parents' genes (a two-point crossover). Each child's genes then have a 10% chance of mutating to prevent stagnation.

Then the new generation races again, repeating the process ad infinitum. Over time, the cars will (hopefully) evolve into better racers while you listen to Eurobeat.
