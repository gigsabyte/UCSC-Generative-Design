Final Assignment: Purgatory Court

Purgatory Court is a game about studying evidence, finding contradictions, and making objections, forever!

Purgatory Court can be played with only a mouse or touchscreen. A list of places you can click:

  • The textbox will advance text.
  • The Court Record button will display the case's evidence.
  • The evidence buttons will update your current evidence and display its description.
  • The present button will allow you to present evidence during testimony.
    • Make sure to press the button while the Court Record is displayed and your current evidence is selected.

Purgatory Court replicates the “trial” part of a Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney case. It begins in the courtroom, with the player character seemingly waking up in the middle of a trial. You will have a list of generated evidence.

After being given a brief description of the case by the prosecution, a witness will begin providing testimony.You must then find contradictions in the testimony and point them out with evidence.

If you point out all the contradictions and solves the case, another trial will generate itself.
